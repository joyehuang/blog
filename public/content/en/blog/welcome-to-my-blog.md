# Welcome to My Blog

I'm excited to welcome you to my newly redesigned blog! After using Hugo for a while, I've decided to migrate to Next.js to have more control over the design and functionality.

## Why Next.js?

Here are some reasons why I chose Next.js for my blog:

- **Full Control**: With Next.js, I have complete control over the design and functionality
- **React Ecosystem**: Access to the vast React ecosystem and component libraries
- **Performance**: Built-in optimizations for performance and SEO
- **Developer Experience**: Excellent development experience with hot reloading and TypeScript support

## What's New?

This new blog includes several improvements:

1. **Modern Design**: A clean, responsive design that works great on all devices
2. **Dark Mode**: Toggle between light and dark themes
3. **Better Performance**: Optimized for speed and SEO
4. **Enhanced Features**: Better search, categories, and navigation

## What's Coming Next?

I'm planning to add several new features in the coming weeks:

- Search functionality
- Comment system
- Newsletter subscription
- More interactive components

Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you for visiting my blog!
